About us

ECON 91 Ltd., headquartered in Rousse, has been operating since 1991 as a wholly-owned private company. In the 96th year, the country's first private plant for the synthesis of resins started, and in the 98th year production of alkyd and water-dispersed paints began. The company also owns a second production - for mattresses and pilolatex pillows. The production in the town of Byala is unique and the plants with similar capacity are only 15 in the whole world. Byala's products are exported worldwide, with customers in England, Germany, Russia, Italy, the United States, France, Spain, Poland, Greece, Israel and even Korea. ECON is the largest exporter of mattresses from Bulgaria.
The priority of the company is to develop and improve according to the requirements of the modern market, as the leading strategy is the production of products that meet the requirements and needs of consumers. Due to this, as well as continuous reinvestment, today it is an established manufacturer of two main product groups - paint and varnish materials and mattresses and pillows of penolatex.
"ECON" offers a wide range of FORTUNA, PASTELO and KASTELO products: alkyd paints, water-based paints for interior and exterior painting, silicone and polymer plasters, water and alkyd based enamels, all-surface primers, lacquers and wood-based varnishes, , a wide variety of alkyd resins and PVA dispersions, diluents, effective coatings and specialized products. Thanks to PASTELO & FORTUNA Tinting system, the company offers machine tinting of paints and oils in over 20,000 colors. Almost all known catalogs are included in the system, such as NCS, RAL, Color Harmony, NOVA and more.
Paint and varnish products are intended for both mass and industrial consumption - in mechanical engineering, construction, woodworking, etc. Due to their high quality and competitive prices, they are successfully sold not only on the Bulgarian market, but also on the international market, and their interest and demand is constantly growing.
In 2006, a completely new ECON plant was opened in Rousse, which has extremely modern production facilities and owns specialized production equipment of the German company Hageman, unique not only within Bulgaria but also in the Balkans. The machine maintenance and control software, manufactured by HONEYWELL, is specially designed and tailored to the specific features of the plant's equipment. ECON works with raw materials from world-renowned companies such as CLARIANT, BASF, BAYER, KEMIRA, PERSTORP, DOW, BYK, etc., which guarantee the high quality of the finished product. In cooperation with experts from these companies, ECON develops and implements a number of new modern products and technologies in its production.
